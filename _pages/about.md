I’m currently a **Causal Inference Graduate Researcher** at New York University. My work spans statistical modeling, causal inference, and computational social science, often incorporating large-scale simulations, cloud computing, and cutting-edge AI methods.

I completed a **B.A. in Computer Science and Data Science** and currently pursuing a **M.S. in Applied Statistics for Social Science Research** at NYU. My research interests include causal inference, natural language processing, and political behavior analysis.

Outside of research, I enjoy exploring the intersections of data, policy, and society, and translating complex quantitative insights into actionable knowledge.
